The Challenge

Water Crisis

Agriculture consumes 90% of Uzbekistan's fresh water, yet nearly 50% is lost due to inefficient irrigation methods (Source: UN/World Bank).

Financial Gap

Farmers struggle to calculate ROI and fear loans. Banks want to issue "Green Loans" but struggle to assess risk.

The Result

Low adoption of modern farming techniques and high financial stress for small farmers.

Our Solution

A "Pocket Financial & Agronomic Advisor" living inside Telegram.

1

Auto-Location

Share location via Telegram. We instantly detect region, soil type, and weather data.

2

Crop & Tech

Select your crop (e.g., "Cotton") and technology (e.g., "Drip Irrigation").

3

AI Analysis

We calculate expected yield increase, water savings, and ROI.

4

Financial Bridge

Auto-generate a cash-flow calendar and fill an Agrobank loan application.
